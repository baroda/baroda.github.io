Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23/08/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Barodapedia, an application developed by Bank of Baroda. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

2. Data Collection

Personal Data:

We collect the following personal data from our users:

  • Employee Code (EC) Number: Collected during the setup of the 4-digit PIN for login purposes.
  • Mobile Number: Collected during the setup of the 4-digit PIN for login purposes.

Non-Personal Data:

We collect non-personal data for analytics and usage tracking, including:

  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the app, such as pages viewed, actions performed, etc.
  • Device Information: Information about the device used to access the app, including OS version, device type, etc.

3. Use of Data

Personal Data is used to facilitate secure login and provide personalized services within the app.

Non-Personal Data is used to improve the app's performance, user experience, and to understand user behavior.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. This includes encryption of sensitive information and secure storage practices.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Personal Data is retained as long as you use the app or until you request deletion.

Non-Personal Data may be retained for analytics purposes.

7. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. Please contact us at cs.ho@bankofbaroda.com to exercise your rights.

8. Changes to this Policy
